ABOUT US

What is Carry Your Cross about?

Carry Your Cross is a Christian branded store founded in 2026, created to spread the gospel through our products. We noticed the shortage of Christian branded apparel in Botswana and took it upon ourselves to solve that issue by offering faith-inspired designs.

Our Vision

To empower the youth across Botswana to spread the gospel through quality branded apparel. Our goal is to help customers bring the reality of God to their world — if they cannot say it, they can wear it.

The Face Behind Carry Your Cross

Michael Mothusi Mmusi

Michael is the founder of Carry Your Cross. He grew up surrounded by the four walls of a church, serving as a keyboardist and youth coach. His passion to see the youth empowered to share what they learn in church inspired him to bring Carry Your Cross to life.
